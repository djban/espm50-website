Usage Instructions

Hover over each state to view a quick overview of the state's carbon emissions. This includes the Percent GDP per CO2 (how much GDP the state is gaining per tonne of CO2), the state's major exports, and total CO2 in tons.

Click on each state itself to open a new window with the state's individual webpage depicting detailed annual carbon emissions trends and a brief summary on the state's current carbon emissions status.

Scroll with your mouse or use the + and - buttons to zoom in and out of the map. Note that certain smaller states have dedicated buttons located on the right side of the map for click accessibility.

Interactive Map

In the above interactive map, the color of each state reflects the interdependency of that state's industries on carbon emissions. The darker the state's color, the less GDP per ton of CO2 the state produces. The data shown comes from a 2021 dataset that provides state-level numerical information about carbon emissions.
