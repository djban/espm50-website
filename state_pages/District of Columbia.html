District of Columbia Carbon Emissions

The District of Columbia, as the capital of the United States, has a diverse and dynamic economy. While not a state, it plays a significant role in influencing national policies, including those related to the environment. The district is committed to sustainability, with initiatives focused on renewable energy, energy efficiency, and green infrastructure. Efforts to reduce carbon emissions and enhance urban resilience are integral to the district's culture, reflecting its role as a hub of political and environmental decision-making.
